What is Basketball?

Basketball is one of the most played sports in the world. It was invented by James Naismith in 1891. It is now a team sport in which two teams, consisting of 5 players each, have the objective of shooting the ball through a circular hoop. Each of the players play one position. The five positions are point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power foward, and center. The most prominent league for basketball is the National Basketball Association, and the face of the NBA is LeBron James.

Basic Skills

There are five basic skills in basketball. The basic skills are:

  • Shooting
  • Dribbling
  • Passing
  • Rebounding
  • Defense
