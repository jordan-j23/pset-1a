Getting Rebounds

Rebounds are when players grab the ball after a player from the opposing team misses a shot. Teams usually have the taller players get rebounds because they are higher in the air. The players that average the most rebounds are usually around 7 feet tall. An exception to that is Russell Westbrook whose athleticism was previously mentioned There is a correct way to getting a rebound.

  1. Bend Knees and Jump
  2. Extend Arms in the Air
  3. Grab Ball with Both Hands and Land on Both Feet
