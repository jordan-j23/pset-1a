Getting Rebounds

Rebounds are when players grab the ball after a player from the opposing team misses a shot. Teams usually have the taller players get rebounds because they are higher in the air. The players that average the most rebounds are usually around 7 feet tall. An exception to that is Russell Westbrook whose athleticism was previously mentioned. Players can also get offensive rebounds. That happens when a player gets a rebound from a shot their teammate missed. Players who get a lot of offensive rebounds are usually unappreciated by teammates and fans. Despite what you may think, there is a correct way to getting a rebound.

    The steps to getting a rebound are:
  1. Box-out Opponent
  2. Bend Knees and Jump
  3. Extend Arms in the Air
  4. Grab Ball with Both Hands and Land on Both Feet
